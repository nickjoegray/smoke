Mission
Through my years of experience working in both franchise and e-commerce industries, I understand the value of and importance of adopting a user-first approach. This approach aims to build an experience suited to meet the needs of the consumer from conception to development. I am a motivated, forward-thinking, passionate and persevering young adult who has taken on many valuable experiences in my recent business endeavours. In whichever environment I am immersed in, I take the time to learn about the company's culture and structure in order to assist in growing the company and enhancing team cohesiveness.
I first learned about the concept of putting the user first when I took on the role of West Coast Operations with Wild Wing restaurants where I grew the brand through locations, franchisees and licensing boards within four provinces. There, I was dealing with a broad range of clientele from franchisees to restaurant customers to real estate agents to marketing executives , each with unique needs and specifications. Thus, it was critical for me to develop a clear understanding of who the consumer was to best serve their needs, while remaining true to the Wild Wing brand.
I then took all of my knowledge about creating a user-first approach to HomeSav , an e-commerce company specializing in home decor, working as a Junior Software Developer. When I was brought on at HomeSav, I made it my priority to gain a rich understanding of the company's mission, values and staff makeup by spending time within each department and then learning about interactions between them. As I was responsible for designing and leading email marketing campaigns and developing the user interface, my expertise in consumer-driven experience was brought to life. I gained insight about HomeSav's users through Google Analytics, A/B Testing and Customer Feedback which allowed me to suit each task to the needs of the consumers.
I continue to hone in these skills each day through personal development, meetings with business professionals, reading and market research. I am always looking to grow myself to better help the next brand. I am a team player who enjoys working with others and expanding my knowledge base around different roles within whichever company I am working with. Although I am a young professional who is still developing and growing everyday, I make every effort to bring the user to the forefront. Who is your ideal user?

Alexander Norman. CEO & Co-founder, HomeSav.
